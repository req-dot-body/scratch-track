About ScratchTrack


Manage your songwriting workflow on the go


ScratchTrack is designed for easily capturing musical ideas on the go and for managing your songwriting workflow.

Our interface allows a user to quickly record at the click of a button, to write and revise lyrics, and to transcribe guitar tablature into a clean, organized format legible by other musicians.

With ScratchTrack you can generate ideas, revisit past songs, and explore other users' projects to find some inspiration.


Never let a song idea idea slip through your fingers again.
